Introducing Computers in Ontario’s Classrooms

Provincial Ambitions, Teacher Preparation, and Technological Change in the 1980s

I’m conducting research into how Ontario teachers shared information in preparation for the wide-scale introduction of standardized educational computers in the 1980s and early 1990s.

As part of my documentation, I’m publishing relevant notes, findings, and thoughts from the research process. I’m slowly adding to this section, transferring previously local documents to this site. Consider this an incomplete record of my research process, with some parts better noted than others.
