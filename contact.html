Contact

Human contact makes me happy.

No matter the reason, I’d love to hear from you. You can find me on a variety of digital platforms:

  • Email
  • Twitter (on hiatus)
  • Facebook (checking infrequently)

Use whichever you’re most comfortable with. I’ll answer pretty promptly. I also enjoy mail, phone calls, and meeting in person, but prefer that we arrange those digitally.

I also publish a weekly newsletter, where I write about whatever’s on my mind in a given week. This usually comes from whatever I’m reading, and usually has some connection to the intersection of technology and society. If that interests you, or you’d like to know more, please check out the subscription page.
